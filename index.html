My Personal understanding of Yoga.

Yoga is about finding balance between the body and mind. It's a physical, mental and even spiritual exersise You don't need to be Spiricoco before you can do yoga. lol.
It's a personal journey which is more than just putting the body in 'weird' positions. These positions are called ASANAS.

Yoga has origins from Ancient India. Anybody can do Yoga, young or old, overweight, underweight or fit , male or female. Think of it as your regular body fitness exercise.

Male Yoga practitioners are referred to as YOGIS while the female practitoners are YOGINIS.

Personally, I don't like labels so everyone is a YOGI to me.

YOGA ASANAS

Asana means position, posture or pose.

There are so many different positions (poses), from Beginner to Advanced level. No Asana is less important than the other; all that matters is to feel it in the right places.

Some Yoga Poses

It's a not really a good idea for a Beginner.

RISKS OF YOGA

This life no balance. No matter how good something is, too much of it could be dangerous too.

  • Injury - Getting into a pose that your body isn't ready for could cause serious injury.
    This is why it's advisable to start with beginner friendly poses and not push your body too hard to get into a pose. With time, your body adjusts.
  • Pregnancy - Yoga during pregnancy should be done with extra care, it's advisable to have a someone around just incase.
  • Medical conditions - If you have any medical condition, you should consult a Medical Practitioner before starting yoga. To avoid stories that touch.

Remember to practise safely.
